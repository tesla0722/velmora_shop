Size guide

How to choose your size when shopping online—same approach used by leading apparel retailers.

  1. Measure yourself with a soft tape: bust/chest, waist, hips, inseam for pants.
  2. Compare to the chart on each product page (we list garment measurements, not just body ranges).
  3. Between sizes? Size up for a relaxed fit; size down for a closer cut on stretch fabrics.

Still unsure? Contact us with your measurements—we’re happy to recommend a size before you order.
